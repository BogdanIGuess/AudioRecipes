Low calorie greek yogurt salad dressing

sam
    • Ingredients:
      1. 2 cloves garlic
      2. 280 grams of greek yogurt
      3. 1 tbsp of oil
      4. 1 tbsp vinegar
      5. salt to taste
      6. water to desired consistency
    • Recipe:
      mix greek yogurt , oil and vinegar. Mince the garlic, or use a ricer or food processor, until fine. Mix with the yogurt base (if using food processor, add the base to mix thoroughly). Add salt to taste and water until desired thickness.
