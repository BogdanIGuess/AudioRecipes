Sweet Potato Gnocchi

Sweet potato gnocchi
    • Ingredients:
      1. 1 sweet potato
      2. flour, until not sticky
      3. salt
    • Recipe:
      Using a fork, stab holes in the sweet potato before putting it in the oven at 200 degrees celcius for about 30 minutes or until tender. Remove the skin and mash, mixing with flour and a touch of salt until its no longer sticky. Roll into long, thick strands, cut into small bits and boil in salted water until they start floating to the top.
